Structure

The structure is the shell of the tiny house and it is made up of the floor system, wall system, and roof system. This sections provides design options for different structural systems and associated constrcution details.

Electric Power

Electricity for tiny house can either be grid connected or off-grid. Off-grid systems include solar-electric, wind-electric, or wind-solar hybrid. This section will cover solar electric system componenets and desing and provide design options for grid-connected system layouts.

Water Treatment

Water treatment and sanitation can either utlize grid-connected systems though municiple infrastructure or off-grid systems through rainwater catchment systems and composting toilets. We will discuss in-depth off-grid solutions and provide design options for grid-connected system layouts.

Appliances

Due to space limitation and reduced energy consumption, tiny houses rely on transforming furniture and energy efficient appliances. This section will provide a survey of furniture and appliances specifically selected for tiny house design.
